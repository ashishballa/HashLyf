DEPLOYMENT OPTIONS FOR YOUR LIFE INSURANCE APP
===============================================

Option 1: VERCEL (Recommended - Free & Easy)
--------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Sign up with GitHub, Google, or email
3. Click "Import Project" or "Add New Project"
4. Upload this entire folder OR connect from GitHub
5. Vercel will auto-detect Next.js settings
6. Click "Deploy"
7. Get instant URL like: your-app-name.vercel.app

Option 2: NETLIFY (Also Free)
-----------------------------
1. Go to https://netlify.com
2. Drag and drop this entire project folder
3. Wait for deployment
4. Get URL like: your-app-name.netlify.app
